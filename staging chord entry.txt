--- Additional clarification on enumerated ratios ---

for assistance on how to interpret enumerated ratios into a a string of HEJI note names:

if base freq = n/d
and chord = a:b:c: ...

find common denominator of a:bc:...

multiply each of those ratios by n/d

ex: base freq = 5/6
chord = 4:5:6

HEJI Output displays the note names for the three ratios 5/6, 25/24, and 5/4
